My Project Objective !

Online HealthCare management System

Details

• This will bring all the hospitals in one platform. It will make the largest hospital network in Bangladesh. So that, the patients can easily access to these hospitals.
• This hospital network will unify all the hospitals and applicants together.
• That offers several tools to allow the patients to view the complete information of the existing hospitals and service process.
• Hospitals can attract the patients by providing discounts, presenting their specialists, special service facilities, etc. in their newsfeed
• And also will offer the standards of the hospitals in Bangladesh.
• patients can choice the hospitals with own interest.
• So in this way, we can make a comfortable connection among the hospitals and the patients.

So what made me motivated to create this application?

• I didn’t find such type of digital healthcare management application in Bangladesh. But it is today’s demand. As Smartphones and tabs have made life easier, So why not in healthcare management. • There is No easy platform to view the complete information of the existing hospitals and service process. So I felt it will be a good idea to make such hospital network. • Another issue but very important one is- it is Difficult to recognize the standards of the hospitals in Bangladesh. There are a lot of disqualified, unauthorized, fake hospitals and there is not much steps against them. So there is a need to create a platform where the patients can find the standards from previous feedback.

So what do we need today?

• Today We need a Smart online checkout hospital service reliability • Which will Offer several tools to view the complete hospital information • The patients can be able to rate the hospital performances. • A network is needed which helps access to patient waveforms at anytime from anywhere. • A Better communication among the hospitals and the patients is truly needed in Bangladesh. • Applicants can get Current information of hospital services. • And Contact information of the specialists in a specific hospital. • all these services will improve patient safety. • and will give the patients Faster search time.

This connection will ensure a hassle-free application process.
